Our Journey

Potlapalli Farm began in 2009 as a dream — to create a farm where animals are treated with respect, food is grown without chemicals, and families can trust what they eat.



Located in the serene countryside of Andhra Pradesh, Potlapalli Farm began in 2009 with a simple dream — to create a place where animals are cared for with respect, food is grown naturally, and families can trust what they eat. We focus on sustainable and ethical farming practices, raising free-range goats, native chickens, and dairy animals without harmful chemicals or artificial methods. Our goal is to provide pure, nutritious products straight from our farm to your home.



Today, we deliver fresh produce directly to homes — cutting out middlemen, reducing carbon footprint, and bringing farm-fresh goodness to your table.



Every drop of milk, every egg, every cut of meat is handled with care — because we believe food should be honest, healthy, and full of life.

Mr. Potlapalli Balakrishna

The Visionary

Mr. Potlapalli Balakrishna was born in “Pullalacheruvu” village, Prakasam district, Andhra Pradesh, in 1964. The youngest of five brothers, he was raised in a family deeply rooted in agriculture. His ancestors migrated from Warangal and served as custodians of land and tradition through generations.



He completed his schooling at ZPHS Pullalacheruvu, earned a B.Sc. in Agriculture from Hindu College, Guntur, and pursued higher studies in History, Political Science, and English from Osmania and Kakatiya Universities. Though he aimed for civil services, life led him toward education and entrepreneurship.



In Hyderabad, he built a career in teaching and real estate, but always carried the dream of returning to the soil. His belief in "work is worship" inspired the creation of Potlapalli Farm — a legacy rooted in values, knowledge, and nature.



Today, his vision guides every decision — ensuring that Potlapalli Farm remains a symbol of integrity, sustainability, and rural empowerment.

Potlapalli Venkateshwara Rao

The Founder

Potlapalli Venkateshwara Rao is the driving force behind Potlapalli Farm, blending his precision as a Quality Control professional at Dr. Reddy’s Laboratories with a deep passion for sustainable farming.



What began as a dream to provide chemical-free, honest food has grown into a trusted farm brand. His scientific approach ensures every product meets the highest standards of hygiene, safety, and nutrition.



Even while managing a demanding corporate career, he has dedicated himself to building the farm from the ground up — overseeing animal care, feed quality, and delivery logistics with unwavering commitment.



Under his leadership, Potlapalli Farm delivers freshness, transparency, and health to every household. His journey proves that with passion and perseverance, farming can be both noble and modern.
