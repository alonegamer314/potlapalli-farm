Our Journey

Potlapalli Farm began in 2009 as a dream — to create a farm where animals are treated with respect, food is grown without chemicals, and families can trust what they eat.



Located in the serene countryside of Andhra Pradesh, Potlapalli Farm began in 2009 with a simple dream — to create a place where animals are cared for with respect, food is grown naturally, and families can trust what they eat. We focus on sustainable and ethical farming practices, raising free-range goats, native chickens, and dairy animals without harmful chemicals or artificial methods. Our goal is to provide pure, nutritious products straight from our farm to your home.



Today, we deliver fresh produce directly to homes — cutting out middlemen, reducing carbon footprint, and bringing farm-fresh goodness to your table.



Every drop of milk, every egg, every cut of meat is handled with care — because we believe food should be honest, healthy, and full of life.

Mr. Potlapally Balakrishna

The Visionary

Mr Potlapally Balakrishna born in “Pullalacheruvu” village and Mandal of Prakasham district of Andhra Pradesh in the year 1964. He is the 5thand last son among the brothers. His parents were Sri.Chalamaiah and Smt. Lakshmi Narasamma, hail from agricultural family. Long back ancestors of Potlapally family, migrated from ‘Inavolu’ of Warangal district to a village near Vinukonda of Guntur district. There they formed a village and named after the same village ‘Inavolu’ which is still existing. From there his ancestors worked under the Narsaraopet ‘Zamindar’ as Warriors and revenue personal. After the attacks of “PINDARIES” on the zamindari villages, the leftover people (ancestors of Balakrishna) reached pullalacheruvu village. In fact it was a pulula cheruvu, where the Tigers were resting on the banks of the cheruvu called Kataya vemareddy cheruvu.



Mr Potlapally Balakrishna completed schooling from ZPHS Pullalacheruvu. 12th (Bi.P.C) from A.C College, Guntur. Graduation B.Sc. with agriculture as specialisation from Hindu College Guntur. Attempted for UPSC examination – Indian Forest Service as well as Indian Administrative Service. In the meantime, he got B.A from O.U. Also M.A (history) M.A (political science) from O.U. and M.A (English) through SDLCE of Kakatiya University. Due to unavoidable circumstances, he could not complete his final year LLB from PRR Law College, Hyderabad.



Thereafter settled in Hyderabad, in teaching profession, for a long time. Apart from teaching, he used to work in real estate and marketing area. As a hope, having high spirits of individualities, he started his own educational institutions as well as real estate ventures.



After gaining sufficient experience and strength, he set his goals higher and higher. In reaching higher goals and for his satisfaction, he initiated the said areas of business firms. His ultimate slogan, he always believes in “work is worship”
